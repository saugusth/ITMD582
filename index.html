My Top 5 Animated Movies

Number 5: Planet Hulk

After the Hulk becomes too much for the Avengers to handle, he is sent into space. He lands on a planet ruled by the red king and is forced to be a gladiator. With the help of new frends, can he free himself of slavery? This is a good movie for people who want to see Hulk fight with both his fist and his brain.
